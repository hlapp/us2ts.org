Program

Posted on 2018, Nov 28

Latest: All presentation slides are available at US2TS Slides

As a symposium, the focus of US2TS will be on discussions and community building, and thus there is no paper track. However, everybody is invited and encouraged to bring an A1-sized poster to the poster session. Please indicate during the registration whether you plan on bringing a poster as space is limited. The program will run from 8:30am-5pm on Monday and Tuesday, and 8:30am-2:30pm on Wednesday.

Challenges in Semantic Web :
Submit challenges or issues with semantic technologies using this form. We will use your input to discuss current challenges and solutions in a plenary session on Wednesday, March 13.

Event locations:
Penn 1 is the main auditorium. Penn 2 is adjacent to it on the same level. Penn Garden is one level below.
Reception at The Cookery, which is located at 1101 West Chapel Hill Street, Durham, NC, 27701.
